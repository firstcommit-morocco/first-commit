# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **FirstCommit Morocco**, pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, inclusive, and welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, education, background, identity, or personal characteristics.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being kind and respectful to others
- Helping beginners and answering questions politely
- Giving constructive feedback
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting different viewpoints and experiences

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
- Discrimination or hateful language
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Sharing private information of others without permission
- Any behavior that makes others feel unsafe or unwelcome

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including:

- GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Comments and reviews
- Any official community channels linked to this project

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.

If unacceptable behavior occurs, maintainers may take actions such as:

- Asking for a change in behavior
- Removing comments or contributions
- Closing issues or pull requests
- Temporarily or permanently banning a contributor from the project

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers by opening an issue titled:

**"Code of Conduct Report"**

(If you prefer privacy, you can contact the maintainer directly through GitHub.)
